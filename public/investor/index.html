Valuation Methodology

Equity (SAFEs, priced rounds, convertible notes): fair value equals transaction price (cost basis) unless a triggering event has occurred — a new priced round in the portfolio company, a material adverse event, or a third-party secondary at a materially different price. At a triggering event, the position is marked to the new observable price or an impairment is documented. No DLOM is applied to equity.

Pre-TGE tokens (SAFTs, token warrants, pre-launch): fair value equals transaction price (cost basis) until token generation event or a subsequent priced round. Same triggering-event logic as equity.

Liquid / fully vested tokens: fair value equals the spot price of the token at the measurement date, sourced from CoinGecko sampled at page load on the reporting date.

Locked or vesting tokens (with liquid underlying market): fair value equals spot × (1 − DLOM), applied tranche-by-tranche based on each tranche's remaining restriction period. Discount for lack of marketability is calibrated to Longstaff-style protective put option pricing model outputs at crypto-typical volatility (~80–140% annualized):

0–1 year remaining30% 1–2 years remaining45% 2–3 years remaining55% 3+ years remaining65%

Strategic allocations: where a strategic token grant accompanies a cash investment (Hyperbeat, ETHGas), the tranches are reported as a single "Investment" line. Cash deployed reflects only the paid portion; token and equity percentages aggregate paid + granted. MOIC captures total cash-on-cash return inclusive of the strategic grant.

Entry FDV: for strategic positions, this is a blended FDV — cash deployed ÷ combined token allocation — reflecting the effective entry price across paid + granted tranches. For non-strategic positions, Entry FDV equals the round FDV. For pure-equity positions, Entry FDV equals the round equity FDV.

Current FDV: live token FDV where a token trades publicly (price × total supply), otherwise the most recent round valuation.

Exceptions: for any position where trading volume is thin relative to the Fund's holding, or where protocol-specific, regulatory, or concentration risks are not captured by the tiered table, additional judgment is applied and documented in a per-position fair value memo.

Vesting: computed from each position's contractual schedule against today's date. Charts show cumulative percent unlocked from TGE/effective date through full unlock.

Prices: fetched live from CoinGecko on page load. If the API is unavailable, last-known prices are used.
