Valuation Methodology

Strategic allocations (Hyperbeat, ETHGas): cash deployed purchases a paid tranche; a strategic grant adds additional token + equity allocation at no cost. The two tranches are reported as a single "Investment" line; cash reflects only the paid portion while token and equity percentages aggregate paid + granted. Current Mark = cash (equity at cost) + combined token allocation × current FDV/price, so MOIC captures the strategic bonus plus ongoing token performance.

Non-strategic SAFE + Token Warrant / SAFT (Rise, Resolv): cash was the combined basis for equity + token warrant at the round, so Current Mark is not cash + tokens (that double-counts the warrant). Instead, Current Mark = cash × (current FDV ÷ entry FDV). If the token falls below the round price, MOIC drops below 1.00x; if the next round marks the company up, MOIC scales with the round-to-round multiple.

Pure equity (Symbiotic): single pro-rata claim on the round FDV. Current Mark = cash at cost when entry FDV equals current FDV; MOIC stays at 1.00x until a subsequent valuation signal moves.

Entry FDV: for strategic positions, blended FDV = cash ÷ combined allocation (reflects the effective entry price across paid + granted tranches). For non-strategic positions, Entry FDV equals the round FDV. For pure-equity positions, Entry FDV equals the round equity FDV.

Current FDV: live token FDV where a token trades publicly (price × total supply), otherwise the most recent round valuation. No markup is taken between rounds.

Discounted Mark / Discounted MOIC: applies a tiered discount for lack of marketability (DLOM) tranche-by-tranche to each vesting unlock, calibrated to Longstaff-style protective put option pricing model outputs at crypto-typical volatility (~80–140% annualized) per the Fund's ASC 820 / AICPA Valuation Guide (2019) policy.

0–1 year remaining30% 1–2 years remaining45% 2–3 years remaining55% 3+ years remaining65%

Each tranche receives the tier matching its specific remaining lockup; the portfolio's effective DLOM is the weighted average of those tier DLOMs by tranche size. Vested/liquid tokens receive no DLOM. For strategic positions the DLOM applies only to the locked token portion (equity held at cost is not further discounted); for non-strategic positions the whole mark is discounted since it derives entirely from locked-token FDV. Pure-equity positions use a per-position flat haircut documented as an exception, since policy does not apply DLOM to equity.

Vesting: computed from each position's contractual schedule against today's date. Charts show cumulative percent unlocked from TGE/effective date through full unlock.

Prices: fetched live from CoinGecko on page load. If the API is unavailable, last-known prices are used.
